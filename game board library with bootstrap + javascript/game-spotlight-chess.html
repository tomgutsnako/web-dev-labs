Game Spotlight: Chess

A timeless battle of intellect, logic, and creativity.

Classic Rules
Strategic Depth
Global Legacy

Overview

Chess is one of the oldest and most enduring games in the world. Each player controls 16 pieces, aiming to checkmate the opposing king through careful planning and tactics.

Quick Details

  • Origins: India, around the 6th century
  • Players: 2
  • Playtime: 10 minutes – several hours
  • Notable: Recognized as both a game and a sport worldwide
