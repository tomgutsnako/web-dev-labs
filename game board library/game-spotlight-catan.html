Overview

Catan is a modern classic where players gather resources, trade with each other, and build settlements and roads to score victory points. Balance resource generation and negotiation to outpace opponents — first to the target points wins.

Quick Details

  • Designer: Klaus Teuber
  • Players: 3–4 (expandable)
  • Playtime: ~60–120 minutes
  • First released: 1995
