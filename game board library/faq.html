Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a board game?

Visit our Games section, choose a title, and follow the borrowing instructions listed there.

Can I suggest a new game to add?

Yes! Go to the Contact page and send us your suggestions. We love hearing from you.

Do you offer tutorials?

Absolutely. Check out the How to Play section for detailed guides and videos.

Are there membership fees?

No, our library is free to explore. Some events may have small entry fees to support community activities.
