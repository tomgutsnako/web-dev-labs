Overview

In Splendor, players act as Renaissance merchants acquiring mines, transportation, and artisans to produce precious gems. The goal is to attract noble patrons by building an efficient engine of gem production and trade.

Quick Details

  • Designer: Marc André
  • Players: 2–4
  • Playtime: ~30 minutes
  • Released: 2014
