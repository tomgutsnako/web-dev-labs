Overview

Chess is one of the oldest and most enduring games in the world. Each player controls 16 pieces, aiming to checkmate the opposing king through strategy, tactics, and careful planning. Played for centuries, chess combines elegance with endless complexity.

Quick Details

  • Origins: India, around the 6th century
  • Players: 2
  • Playtime: ~10 minutes to several hours
  • Notable: Recognized as both a game and a sport worldwide
